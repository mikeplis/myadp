Welcome to MyADP!

Create your own custom ADP reports

Select "Custom" from the dropdown above, and then input the year and league ID of the MFL leagues you want to use to generate your ADP report. Then, you'll be taken to a page with a unique URL that you can bookmark and return to whenever you want, without having to input your league data again.

Filter by name, position, or team

Use the search box and dropdowns at the top of each column to view only the players you want to view. You can even find all rookies by selecting "FA" from the team filter.
